DashNote

Visual identity concepts for the premium note-taking browser extension. Every icon explores a different writing metaphor.

01
Folded Corner
Warm white page with a classic diagonal dog-ear and subtle ruled lines.
02
Pencil Stroke 'D'
Textured graphite 'D' with varying stroke weights and an underline.
03
Ruled Notebook
Open notebook spread on deep forest green with visible center binding.
04
Bullet Journal
Minimalistic geometric symbols representing task states (Done, To-Do, Cancelled).
05
Sticky Note
Vibrant tilted yellow note with a lift-effect shadow and abstract text lines.
06
Memo Pad
Top-wire spiral binding pad in pale blue with precise professional illustration.
07
Ink Blot Letter
Raw, stamped aesthetic with bleed effects and ink-transfer ruled lines.
08
Margin Notes
Editorial style with a red margin vertical and handwritten asterisk.
09
Torn Paper
High contrast charcoal background with an irregular jagged paper fragment.
10
Stacked Notes
Fanned sequence of paper sheets showing depth and organization.
